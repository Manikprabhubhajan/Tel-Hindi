Shri Manik Prabhu Mandir, Prabhu Nagar

Shri Manik Prabhu Gadhi was established at Nawapet in Vikarabad district of telangana on 12.11.1939 and the temple was constructed in 1948. The unswerving devotion of the devotees and the unwavering love of the people of the village for Prabhu has today made Nawabpet known as the Prabhunagar.
On the occasion of Manikprabhu Jayanti in Margasira Shudha Ekadashi to Padyami of Hindu month regular pujas are performed, Hanuman procession, manikyaprabhu as well as Rakamcharla bhajans, cultural dances are also performed which devotees enjoy, at Prabhu darbar held on concluding day which ends the fair at Sri Sadguru Manikprabhu Mandir in presence of Sarwotmukhyagi Sri Bal Matand Deshpande Maharaj.
Prabhunagar, Nawabpet Mandal
